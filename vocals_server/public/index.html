Features

vocals.ml is a neural network capable of converting your normal voice recording to that of a professional narrator. Using vocals.ml, you can create professional voiceovers, youtube videos, promotional videos, school projects, audiobooks and so much more! It works by using state of the art deep learning techniques to create artificial voice profiles of famous narrators. Then, it is able to cross match your voice with these voice profiles to generate what you said in their voice.

  • Potentially infinite number of voice profiles
  • Non language specific encoding system
  • Speech to Speech conversion (possibly real-time)
  • Add your own voice profiles WIP
